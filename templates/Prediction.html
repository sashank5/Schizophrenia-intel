Prediction of Schizophrenia

Detection of schizophrenia is important because early diagnosis and treatment can lead to better outcomes for those affected. With appropriate care, individuals with schizophrenia can lead fulfilling lives and manage their symptoms, reducing the impact on their daily functioning and overall quality of life. Early detection also helps to reduce the risk of complications such as substance abuse, depression, and suicide. Furthermore, prompt diagnosis and treatment can prevent unnecessary suffering and reduce the burden on families and the healthcare system.
